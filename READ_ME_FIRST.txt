===================================================================
  EV LAB DASHBOARD  -  SETUP ON YOUR RASPBERRY PI  (read this first)
===================================================================

You only do this ONCE, on a brand-new Pi. It takes 20-40 minutes and
needs an internet connection. After it finishes, the Pi will always
open the app by itself when you switch it on.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1.  Extract the downloaded ZIP into your Home folder.
         You should end up with a folder named  EV_Chatbot_Pi5
         sitting directly inside your Home folder. That's all that
         matters - it works for any Pi username automatically.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2.  Open that folder and double-click:
                 "INSTALL EV Lab Dashboard (first time)"

         - If the Pi asks what to do, choose  "Execute in Terminal".
         - If it asks for a password, type your Pi login password.
         - A black window shows the progress. Leave it alone until done.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3.  When it finishes, the Pi restarts on its own and opens the
         app full screen. That's it - setup is complete.

===================================================================
EVERYDAY USE
===================================================================
  * Turn the Pi ON  -> the app opens full screen automatically.
  * Exit the app to the desktop ->  press   Ctrl + Alt + X
  * Re-open the app from the desktop -> double-click the
        "EV Lab Dashboard"  icon.
===================================================================
